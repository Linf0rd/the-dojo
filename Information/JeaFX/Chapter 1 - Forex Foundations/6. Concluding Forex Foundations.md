
###### What is forex?

- The foreign exchange market.

###### Who trades forex?

- Retail traders, banks, hedge funds, trading firms, etc.

###### When can we trade?

- The markets are open 24/5 closing only for the weekend.

###### When should we be trading?

- The best time to trade is through the London & New York sessions as the overlap of the sessions has the highest volatility before the market begins slowing down.
